What is Dragon Quest?

Dragon Quest is a walking event with a difference - not only do teams compete on their navigation and timekeeping abilities, but they also compete in numerous small skill-based challenges at various points along the route. Points are awarded for teamwork, walking skills, and challenge performance. The winning team not only gets the glory of winning, but also our beautiful Dragon trophy for the year.

When is Dragon Quest?

Dragon Quest is held at a weekend in September or October. This year, we're letting leaders who register their interest early vote for dates. You can vote with this form.

Where is Dragon Quest?

The location of this year's event is yet to be decided, but it will be somewhere in the West Pennine Moors.

Who can do Dragon Quest?

Dragon Quest is now open to Scouts (10 - 14) from all across West Lancashire! You'll need between 4 and 7 people to form a team.

Where do I sign up?

Registration will open nearer to the event, but leaders can register their interest to be notified when registration is available using this form.

How much does Dragon Quest cost?

The cost for 2025 is yet to be calculated, but previous events have cost between £10 and £15 per team.

What do I need to bring?

A full kit list is available here. Kit will be checked before the teams set off. Also, please make sure you bring a completed parental consent form for every young person taking part.

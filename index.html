Welcome to 1st New Longton Scouts, we are a Scout group offering fun, challenge and adventure to young people aged 6-18. We aim to build the confidence of young people and develop skills that will stay for life. Whether you are a young person looking to join or an adult looking to volunteer come and give Scouting a go in New Longton!

Cubs is for children aged 8 - 10.5, we meet on a Monday evening 18:30 - 20:00. We enjoy going camping and trying lots of activities for the first time - this might be zip-wiring, archery or even caving! We also learn lots of new skills, earn lots of badges and help others in our community.
